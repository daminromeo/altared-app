Brand Overview

About Altared

Altared is a modern wedding planning platform that helps couples compare vendors, scan proposals with AI, and manage their wedding budget in one place. Our brand reflects elegance, warmth, and clarity — making the overwhelming process of wedding planning feel organized and beautiful.

Brand Values

Elegant

Refined and sophisticated without being pretentious. We celebrate the beauty of wedding planning.

Warm

Approachable and supportive. We're a trusted companion on your journey, not a cold tool.

Clear

Simple and organized. We bring clarity to complex decisions with clean, intuitive design.

Brand Voice

Tone Guidelines

Do

  • Use warm, encouraging language
  • Keep copy concise and scannable
  • Celebrate milestones with the couple
  • Use "your wedding" not "the wedding"
  • Lead with benefits, not features

Don't

  • Use overly corporate or technical language
  • Create urgency or pressure
  • Use gendered assumptions
  • Over-use exclamation marks
  • Reference competitor brands
